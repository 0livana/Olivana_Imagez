About Me

Hi, my name is Oliva Nana

I capture & curate visual stories causing you to linger.

I am passionate about crafting contexts where people flourish through curating, cultivating and capturing stories. Watching the world move captivates and inspires me. I love finding unique and creative ways to curate the stories around me into visual narratives. Every element of photography is centered around people. Even when there is no human to be seen, the image itself is designed to engage someone's eye. People are the focal point and it's light that captures them.

Born in the City of Benin, Edo State. I grew up watching the sun rise and set anf the elegance of nature. As the swell rose and fell, the light danced around me and those I was with. It was here my curiosity and passion for light was formed. When the camera first landed in my hands the transition was seamless.

I am lifestyle photographer now live in Asaba, Delta State and work as a Freelancer in Photography and Web-Development. I process the world in pictures, looking forward to capturing your special day for eternity!
